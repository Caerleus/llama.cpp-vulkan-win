# Screenshots

This folder contains visual proof and examples of the software in action.

## What you will find here:
* **Vulkan Detection:** Screenshots of the terminal showing the GPU being correctly recognized by the Vulkan backend.
* **Inference Speed:** Images showing the tokens-per-second (t/s) performance during a live session.
* **Web UI:** Examples of how the llama-server interface looks when running in your browser.

## Contributing
If you achieve great results with a specific GPU, feel free to take a screenshot of your terminal/UI and share it! This helps other users understand what performance to expect from similar hardware.
